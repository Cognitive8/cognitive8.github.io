Welcome to Cognitive 8

Here, you can learn about the cognitive processes that make up your cognitive type. You can also learn how to identity the eight cognitive processes in yourself and others.

Each cognitive process is internal, though it does have external manifestations. Through these consistent and reliable subconscious cues, we're able to determine cognitive type.
